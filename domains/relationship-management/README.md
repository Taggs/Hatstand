# Relationship Management

## Overview

The relationship-management domain covers customer relationship tracking, CRM integration, customer lifecycle analytics, and the relationship manager operating model for commercial and corporate customers.

## Boundaries

### This Domain Covers

- Customer relationship data and 360-degree view
- Relationship manager assignment and coverage model
- Customer interaction tracking and history
- Next-best-action and cross-sell recommendations
- Customer satisfaction and NPS tracking
- CRM system integration
- Customer segmentation and tiering
- Relationship review workflows

### This Domain Does NOT Cover

- Customer acquisition and onboarding (see `onboarding`)
- Product-specific servicing (see respective product domains)
- Marketing campaigns and communications (see `customer-communications` — TBD)
