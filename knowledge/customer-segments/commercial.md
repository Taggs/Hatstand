# Commercial Customer Segment

## Definition

Mid-market businesses with 250-2,000 employees and annual turnover between EUR 50 million and EUR 500 million.

## Key Characteristics

- Dedicated relationship manager expected
- Multi-entity, multi-jurisdiction structures common
- Sophisticated treasury and cash management needs
- Regulatory reporting obligations (e.g., large exposure reporting)
- Integration via host-to-host, SWIFT, and APIs

## Products and Services

- Complex current account structures (pooling, sweeping)
- Cash management and liquidity solutions
- Trade finance (letters of credit, guarantees, documentary collections)
- Supply chain finance
- Foreign exchange and hedging
- Structured lending and syndicated facilities
- Securities services
- Corporate cards and expense management

## Relevant Domains

- `account-servicing` — Multi-entity account structures
- `lending` — Structured and syndicated lending
- `payments` — High-value and cross-border payments
- `credit-decisioning` — Complex credit assessment
- `relationship-management` — Dedicated RM model
- `beyond-banking` — Value-added advisory services
