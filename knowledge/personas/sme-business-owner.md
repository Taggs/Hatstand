# SME Business Owner

## Profile

- **Segment**: Small and Medium Enterprise (1-250 employees)
- **Role**: Owner, director, or financial controller
- **Digital literacy**: Moderate to high
- **Primary channels**: Internet banking, mobile app, relationship manager

## Goals

- Manage business cash flow efficiently
- Access credit quickly when needed
- Minimise time spent on banking administration
- Separate business and personal finances clearly

## Pain Points

- Slow credit decisioning processes
- Limited self-service options for business accounts
- Complex multi-signatory approval workflows
- Poor integration with accounting software

## Banking Needs

- Business current accounts
- Business credit cards
- Overdrafts and revolving credit
- Term loans and asset finance
- Trade finance (import/export)
- Payroll processing
- Merchant services (card acceptance)

## Key Journeys

1. Business account opening
2. Applying for business credit
3. Managing payroll and bulk payments
4. Invoice management and reconciliation
5. Foreign currency payments
