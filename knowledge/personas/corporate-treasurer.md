# Corporate Treasurer

## Profile

- **Segment**: Large corporate (250+ employees)
- **Role**: Treasurer, CFO, or senior finance manager
- **Digital literacy**: High
- **Primary channels**: Corporate banking portal, SWIFT, host-to-host, relationship manager

## Goals

- Optimise liquidity and cash positions across entities
- Minimise counterparty and operational risk
- Achieve STP for high-volume transactions
- Maintain full audit trail for compliance

## Pain Points

- Fragmented view across multiple banking relationships
- Manual reconciliation processes
- Limited real-time visibility of global cash positions
- Complex multi-entity, multi-currency structures

## Banking Needs

- Cash management and pooling
- Foreign exchange and hedging
- Trade finance (letters of credit, guarantees)
- Supply chain finance
- Securities services
- Liquidity management
- Host-to-host and API connectivity

## Key Journeys

1. Daily cash position management
2. FX execution and hedging
3. Trade finance instrument issuance
4. Multi-entity payment processing
5. Regulatory reporting and compliance
