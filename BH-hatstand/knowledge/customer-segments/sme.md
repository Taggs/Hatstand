# SME Customer Segment

## Definition

Small and Medium Enterprises with 1-250 employees and annual turnover up to EUR 50 million.

## Sub-Segments

| Sub-Segment | Size | Typical Needs |
|---|---|---|
| Micro | 1-9 employees | Basic current account, card, simple lending |
| Small | 10-49 employees | Cash management, payroll, working capital |
| Medium | 50-250 employees | Multi-account structures, trade finance, FX |

## Key Characteristics

- Price-sensitive but value convenience
- Prefer self-service digital channels
- Credit decisions are time-critical (cash flow dependent)
- Often need integration with accounting software (Xero, QuickBooks, Sage)
- Relationship manager important for medium-sized businesses

## Products and Services

- Business current accounts
- Business savings accounts
- Business credit cards
- Overdrafts
- Term loans
- Asset finance
- Invoice finance
- Merchant services
- Foreign currency accounts and payments
- Business insurance

## Relevant Domains

- `onboarding` — Business account opening and KYC
- `account-servicing` — Account management and statements
- `lending` — Business loans and credit facilities
- `cards` — Business credit and debit cards
- `payments` — Payroll, supplier payments, collections
- `credit-decisioning` — SME credit scoring and approval
