# Corporate Customer Segment

## Definition

Large enterprises with 2,000+ employees and annual turnover exceeding EUR 500 million.

## Key Characteristics

- Senior relationship team (not just one RM)
- Global operations with multi-currency, multi-jurisdiction needs
- High-volume, high-value transaction flows
- Demand for STP and real-time connectivity
- Stringent security, compliance, and audit requirements
- Bespoke pricing and service levels

## Products and Services

- Global cash management and liquidity structures
- Notional and physical pooling across jurisdictions
- Foreign exchange — spot, forward, options, swaps
- Trade finance at scale
- Supply chain finance programmes
- Debt capital markets and structured finance
- Custody and securities services
- Escrow services
- API and host-to-host integration
- Real-time reporting and analytics

## Relevant Domains

- `account-servicing` — Global account structures and reporting
- `lending` — Large-scale and structured facilities
- `payments` — High-value, real-time, cross-border
- `credit-decisioning` — Complex, multi-entity credit assessment
- `relationship-management` — Senior coverage team model
- `platform-engineering` — API connectivity and integration
- `beyond-banking` — Advisory and capital markets access
