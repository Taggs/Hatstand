# Retail Customer

## Profile

- **Segment**: Individual / personal banking
- **Age range**: 18-75
- **Digital literacy**: Varies widely (digital-native to digital-reluctant)
- **Primary channels**: Mobile app, internet banking, branch

## Goals

- Manage day-to-day finances easily
- Access banking services anytime, anywhere
- Understand products and fees clearly
- Feel confident that money and data are secure

## Pain Points

- Complex onboarding processes requiring branch visits
- Unclear fee structures
- Slow or unreliable digital channels
- Difficulty reaching support when needed

## Banking Needs

- Current/savings accounts
- Debit/credit cards
- Personal loans
- Mortgages
- Insurance products
- Investment/savings products

## Key Journeys

1. Account opening and onboarding
2. Day-to-day payments and transfers
3. Applying for credit (loan, mortgage, credit card)
4. Managing cards (activate, freeze, replace)
5. Seeking support (chat, call, branch)
