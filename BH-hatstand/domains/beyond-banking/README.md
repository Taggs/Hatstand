# Beyond Banking

## Overview

The beyond-banking domain covers non-traditional banking services, marketplace offerings, lifestyle services, and value-added products that extend the bank's proposition beyond core financial services.

## Boundaries

### This Domain Covers

- Marketplace and partner product integration
- Lifestyle and rewards offerings
- Advisory services (financial wellness, business advisory)
- Non-financial value-added services
- Ecosystem partnerships and API integrations
- Embedded finance offerings
- Sustainability and ESG-related products

### This Domain Does NOT Cover

- Core banking products (see respective product domains)
- Insurance underwriting (see domain TBD)
- Investment management (see domain TBD)
